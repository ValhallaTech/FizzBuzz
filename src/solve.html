How to use:

  • Change Fizz Divisor and Buzz Divisor to any integers between 2 and 100.
  • Change Count To to set the upper bound of the sequence (max 1000).
  • Click Generate (or press Enter) to produce the sequence.
  • Click Reset Defaults to restore Fizz = 3, Buzz = 5, Count To = 100.
